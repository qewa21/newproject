Snacks & Drinks

Crackers With Peanut Butter

Ingredients:

  • crackers (ideally whole grain)
  • peanut butter

Directions:

  1. Only 1 easy step - spread peanut butter on crackers! This snack has got it all. Whole grain crackers are a good source of fiber and peanut butter gives you plenty of protein and healthy fats. You can also sweeten this snack up with some sliced fresh fruit and drizzle of honey.

Frozen Grapes

Ingredients:

  • grapes (any kind you like!)

Directions:

  1. Put the grapes in a colander and rinse them with cold water for about 30 seconds. Gently rub them as you rinse them.
  2. Check for any broken or decaying grapes and remove them. Gently pull the rest of the grapes from the stem.
  3. Fill the washed and pulled grapes in a ziploc bag.
  4. Freeze the grapes overnight or for at least 8 hours.

Honey Ricotta With Strawberries

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • quarter of a cup of unsalted cashews (change to walnuts or pecans if you prefer something different)
  • 3 cups of strawberries (chopped or halved)

Directions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the ricotta, honey and cashews
  2. Place quarter of a cup of the ricotta mixture and 3 quarters of a cup of strawberries in each of 4 resealable containers
  3. Store the honey ricotta in the same or separate resealable containers for up to 5 days in the fridge
